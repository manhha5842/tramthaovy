SEN

A website/mobile app for a VR folktronica music event that blends contemporary pop music with traditional Vietnamese elements. The Sen will offer an immersive experience, highlighting the fusion of traditional and modern art, and will include a secure and user-friendly ticket purchasing feature

The name "SEN" is inspired by the lotus, symbolism of the Vietnamese woman's strength and resilience. The flower's ability to thrive in muddy waters while maintaining grace and elegance is remarkable. "SEN" is a play on words with the English word "sense", representing subtlety and gentleness in both these Vietnamese and English cultures

Visual Exploration

Maintain the use of traditional neon colors that have come to represent the digital world. These colors should be used in combination to create a high degree of contrast. Incorporate Asian features into the design to reflect the cultural origins of digital technology. "Neon colors" refer to bright, vivid hues that are commonly used in electronic displays and signage. "High contrast" refers to the difference between light and dark areas in an image or design, which can make it easier to read or understand.
