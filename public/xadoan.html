LUNCH when sun up - drunk when sundown

“Xã Đoàn” is a local eatery that focuses on serving lunch and extends its offerings to include alcohol in the evening. Its primary aim is to allure a specific target audience comprising office workers, bank employees, and the like. The overarching goal is to create a place where patrons can not only savor a delicious meal but also unwind and alleviate stress after a challenging day at work.

BRIEFING

With the initial request, "Xa Doan" is a eatery/pub for office workers, more specifically bank employees - a meaningful profession for the owner. Coming to "Xa Doan", not only will delicious lunch be served in the morning but this place will also become an ideal place to relieve stress and meet friends and colleagues.

Using the owner's purpose as an idea, I designed the restaurant's logo as a combination of the main points: banker, lunch, drinks.

Centered around three key concepts—drinks, banks, and lunch—I opted to personify the associated items: beer bottles represent drinks, rolls of money symbolize bankers, and lunch boxes embody the concept of lunch. The expressions and movements assigned to each element are crafted in a positive, joyful, and energetic manner. This design choice is intentional and aimed at perpetuating the upbeat atmosphere that the eatery seeks to cultivate.
